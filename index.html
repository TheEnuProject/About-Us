Steven Eno - Mentor

Steven Eno is El Segundo High School's physics teacher, engineering teacher, and Baseball Coach. As a mentor for the Engineering Design and Development class, he assists with not the problem or the research, but the presentation aspect. Mr. Eno guides the class with giving example presentation videos. Along with that he tries his best to assist students with any advice that he can give as both a teacher and a person.
